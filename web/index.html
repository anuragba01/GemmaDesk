Your Ultimate Offline AI Desk

A powerful, private, multimodal Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) assistant running entirely on your local machine.

Why GemmaDesk?

Complete privacy meets cutting-edge multimodal intelligence.

🔒

100% Offline

No internet required. Your documents, videos, and queries never leave your local machine. Powered by LiteRT and ChromaDB.

🎬

Multimodal & Video

Chat with PDFs, Images, and Videos. Features dynamic temporal clipping that instantly pulls exactly the video frame you ask for.

⚡

Semantic Routing

Smart intent gateways automatically detect if you want a precise answer or a full document summary, adjusting retrieval on the fly.
